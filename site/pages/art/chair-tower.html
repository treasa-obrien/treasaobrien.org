Sculptural installation for ev+a 2001, curated by Salah M. Hassan 

This 4-metre high sculpture intersected the two floors of the Limerick City Gallery of Art and formed a centerpiece for the exhibition in the atrium space. The work, although formal in nature, is based on a fantasy house construction starting with building from objects within domestic space. 

An ostensibly imposing edifice masquerading as whimsy.
