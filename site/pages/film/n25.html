FILM ART WRITING PRESS ABOUT

N25

11mins ‧ Original Format: 16mm film transferred to digibeta, DVD and HDV ‧ England ‧ 2010

 

Bystander society, mutual responsibility, repression and absolution are explored by passengers on a London nightbus 'minding their own business'. 

WINNER: 

Kodak Audience Award at NAHEMI Eat Our Shorts Film Festival, BFI, Southbank, London, 2010. Awarded at Encounters Short Film Festival.
